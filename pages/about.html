About me

Hello, I am Diego, a physicist working in Aichi, Japan.
What it started as a hobby back in undegrad school (~2000) became a profession.
I mostly do data analisis (Python) and recently front-end (JavaScript).

Before joining the recruiting staff company I am currently at, I was working as a Python programmer at the TMC Kamigo Plant.

I received my PhD in Astroparticle Physics from the Graduate School of Science at the University of Nagoya in Japan, under the supervision of Prof. Y. Matsubara and Dr. T. Sako.
My study was generously supported by the Monbukagakusho offered by MEXT, Japan.
My thesis studies 'solar neutrons', which are secondary particles generated after a solar flare occurred. I set upper limits for the ground detection of these particles.

I obtained my M.Sc degree in the Graduate School of Science of Nagoya University.
I finished my thesis on particle identification under the supervision of Prof. Y. Matsubara.
I obtained my B.Sc degree in Physics with a minor in Mathematical methods from San Andres University, Bolivia.
